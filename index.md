# Privacy Policy for Gate

**Last Updated: February 2026**

Gate ("we", "our", "us") operates the Gate mobile application (the "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect:

- Name and email address
- Authentication data (Firebase Authentication)
- Workout session data (exercises, sets, weights)
- Push notification tokens
- Crash diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics)

We do NOT collect:

- Financial information
- Location data
- Browsing history

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Provide workout tracking functionality
- Manage trainer-client sessions
- Send push notifications
- Improve app performance and stability

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

All data is securely stored using Google Firebase services.

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share personal data with third parties.

Data is processed by Google Firebase as infrastructure provider.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active.

Users may request account deletion at any time.
